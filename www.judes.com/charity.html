Committed to
 Giving Back

Here at Jude's family is at the heart of our business and we are committed to giving back to our local and wider community. Alongside our community activities, we have donated over £250,000 to charities that support children and young people.

 

OUR LOCAL COMMUNITY

We work closely with our partner charities Home for Good and Resurgo Spear Programme, raising funds and awareness of their vital work with children and young people.  Our marketing team spent a day with the team at Home for Good, and other members of our team volunteered in running mock interviews with Resurgo. 

We also love to support local charities, churches, food banks and frontline workers with ice cream and treats!  In 2021 we gave away over 180k portions of ice cream, lollies, shakes and sauces in our local community. 

OUR WIDER COMMUNITY

Like the whole world, we have also been heartbroken as we watch events unfold in Ukraine.  We support The Sanctuary Foundation which is working with members of the public, businesses, faith and community groups to coordinate a response.  We have also welcomed 2 Ukrainians to our team here in Twyford.

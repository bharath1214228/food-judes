Our Story

One man dreamed of creating the most unbelievably delicious ice cream.

It came out of the blue, but it made sense. He loved a scoop or two of the good stuff and had often talked about making something with his own two hands; a real product for real people.

He set to work in his small barn in Hampshire and when his wife decided to join him he named the company after her. On a cold winters day in December 2002 he walked across the field to the village pub and sold the very first tub of Jude's ice cream.

Designed for chefs

We were soon working with top chefs around the country, designing new flavours for their menus.

Making our ice cream with the traditional batch method, we carefully select the best ingredients; for example, single estate Colombian cacao, Madagascan bourbon vanilla and fresh milk and cream.

We've tasted and tested literally thousands of recipes over the years and won over 70 Great Taste Awards.

Our Team

To craft brilliant ice cream you need brilliant people. They’re the most important ingredient.

Jude's has been a team effort from day one with family, friends and neighbours working together. We've learnt a lot along the way and, after 20 years, we're proud to have a team of the best ice cream makers in the world.

Community

We believe that all businesses should benefit people and communities.

That's why we are a certified B Corp. We run educational programs with local schools, support community events and have donated over £250,000 to charities that do amazing work, primarily with a focus on children and young people. Find out more here.

PLANET

We are fortunate to live and work in an incredibly beautiful corner of our incredibly beautiful planet. The rolling hills of the South Downs remind us daily that we have a responsibility to protect our natural environment.

We have teamed up with Professor Mike Berners-Lee, one of the world's leading experts in carbon footprinting to make sure that Jude's is reducing carbon intensity as fast as possible. We have a target of a 43% reduction by 2030 from our 2020 base, and have achieved a 20% reduction so far. Read more here.

Grab a scoop

We make take-home tubs in plant based, dairy and lower calorie flavours, as well as lower sugar treats for kids and a growing range of desserts.

So cosy up and tuck into an old favourite, or go wild and try something new.

Our mission is to knock your socks off. Every time.

New Plant Based Flavours!

Chocolate or Salted Caramel?! You decide with our all new Lower Calorie Plant Based Multipack.

Featuring two perfectly portioned tubs each of Lower Calorie Plant Based Double Chocolate ice cream and Lower Calorie Plant Based Salted Caramel ice cream, this new addition forms part of our commitment to make 50% of our retail range plant based by 2025.

The rich and decadent Lower Calorie Plant Based Double Chocolate ice cream is rippled  with indulgent chocolate sauce and delicious dark chocolate curls, and is just 88 kcal per serving. Meanwhile, the ridiculously indulgent Lower Calorie Plant Based Salted Caramel ice cream is swirled through with perfectly balanced, salty-sweet caramel and crunchy honeycomb pieces, with just 93 kcal per serving!

The multipack is exclusively available from Waitrose.

Caramel Cookie Dough Brownie

We’ve taken indulgence to the next level with this new ice cream by taking everyone’s favourites and creating Caramel Cookie Dough Brownie. Crafted with caramel ice cream, a rich caramel ripple and absolutely packed with blonde cookie dough pieces and chocolate brownie pieces, this ice cream is such a treat. In fact, we’ve never seen members of the Jude’s team move so fast to grab another scoop!

Not only does it taste amazing, but since we remove 10% more CO2 from the atmosphere than is emitted through our supply chain, this really is ice cream as you’ve never known it.  Now better for the planet.  

What’s not to like?!

Caramel Cookie Dough Brownie is available from Sainsbury’s.

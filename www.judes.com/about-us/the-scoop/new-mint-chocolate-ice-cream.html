NEW Mint Chocolate ice cream

If you’re looking for an indulgently creamy vegan Mint Chocolate ice cream, then look no further!

Created in collaboration with Summerdown (the peppermint people!), meet our brand new Plant Based Mint Chocolate.  It’s a beautifully British ice cream celebrating the friendship and craftmanship of both Jude’s and Summerdown.

Made using English Black Mitcham peppermint, Mint Chocolate is a rich and creamy, yet delicately flavoured mint ice cream, rippled with a velvety chocolate swirl and rich dark chocolate pieces.

Not only does it taste wonderful, Mint Chocolate also forms part of our commitment to make 50% of our retail range plant based by 2023.

Available exclusively from Ocado and through our online gift shop.

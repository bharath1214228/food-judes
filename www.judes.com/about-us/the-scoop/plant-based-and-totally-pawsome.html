Plant based and totally pawsome!

Treat everyone's favourite family member, the dog, with our new ice cream

As a family of dog-lovers we wanted to ensure that we make the world’s tastiest treats for the whole family, including the pooch. Our latest creation, Ice Cream for Dogs is a plant based strawberry, apple and banana ice cream made from using real fruit purees. Our family Jack Russell Tinker loved being chief taste-tester and can confirm that Ice Cream for Dogs has her full approval!! It’s the perfect treat to be enjoyed on a sunny family day out.

Not only does it taste great, we’re proud to say that we’ve worked with Battersea Dogs and Cats Home to create this delicious and refreshing ice cream, with 5% of sales going to support the wonderful work that Battersea does in helping dogs and cats everywhere.

Ice Cream for Dogs is available from Ocado.

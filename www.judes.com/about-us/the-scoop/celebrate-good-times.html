Celebrate Good Times

It’s our 20th birthday and to mark this oh so special occasion, we’ve delved into our archives for some flavour inspiration and refreshed one of our old favourites, creating our new Limited Edition Plant Based Peach & Champagne ice cream.

With a delicate fruity flavour, Limited Edition Plant Based Peach & Champagne is arriving just in time for a special summer, jam-packed with reasons to raise a class. This truly is the ultimate celebratory ice cream! 

So, whether you’re marking a special anniversary like us, or at a street party for the Queen’s Jubilee weekend, this is one ice cream you won’t want to be without.

This luxurious ice cream is exclusively available through our online shop. To get your hands on one of these limited edition tubs, simply add to a Custom Gift Box and we’ll deliver straight to your door.

And because its vegan, our Plant Based Peach & Champagne is part of our pledge that 50% of our product range will be plant based by 2025.

Summer in a bottle

Say hello to our fruity new Strawberry Coulis – the perfect partner for any summer dessert, and the latest addition to our range of delicious sauces.

Containing real pieces of fresh strawberry, our Strawberry Coulis brings to life the sweet and authentic summery flavour of strawberries. It’s perfect for pouring onto ice cream or drizzling over desserts, adding a touch of summer to any sweet treat.  Try it with our Vanilla Clotted Cream ice cream, a Jude’s take on a British classic. 

Bring on the sunshine!

You can pick up a bottle from Sainsbury's.

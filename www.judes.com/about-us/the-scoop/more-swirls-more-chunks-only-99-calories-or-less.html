MORE SWIRLS. MORE CHUNKS. ONLY 99 CALORIES (OR LESS)!

Lower Calorie Cookies & Cream, Lower Calorie Plant Based Chocolate Brownie, Lower Calorie Plant Based Salted Caramel, Lower Calorie Salted Caramel… need we say more?!  Our newest, most lip-smackingly good lower calorie ice creams are packed with swirls, ripples, honeycomb, cookie and brownie pieces and all 99 calories (or less!) per serving.  With perfectly balanced flavours and no added sugar they are our most delicious lower calorie ice creams yet. 

The 2021 Great Taste Award judges agree awarding new Lower Calorie Salted Caramel, with its caramel swirl and honeycomb pieces, a 1 star Great Taste Award.  

The new range is available in Sainsbury’s, Ocado, Waitrose and Tesco – head into store today.

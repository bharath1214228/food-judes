Our pledge to support Ukrainian refugees

Like the whole world, we have been heartbroken as we’ve seen events unfold in Ukraine. Any of us could be in this unimaginable situation and we can’t just ignore it.

We’re working with The Sanctuary Foundation to coordinate pledges from individuals, businesses, faith and community organisations and more to support Ukrainian refugees as they arrive in the UK. We’ve pledged to host a family, help with their living needs and we’ve already welcomed Vadym into the team here at Jude’s.

The Sanctuary Foundation is a rapid response initiative set up in response to the unfolding humanitarian crisis in Ukraine.

To find out more visit www.sanctuaryfoundation.org.uk.

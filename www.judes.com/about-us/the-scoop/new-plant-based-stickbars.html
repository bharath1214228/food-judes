New Plant Based Stickbars

Our new, ridiculously indulgent and deliciously taste Plant Based Salted Caramel Stickbars have launched just in time for summer. Made with rich and creamy oat-based salted caramel ice cream and rippled with moreish salted caramel sauce, this scrumptious treat is covered in rich chocolate and studded with crunchy honeycomb pieces. Each pack contains three 80ml bars and are the perfect summer treat.

This launch further supports our overall carbon reduction plan, and is aligned with our pledge to make plant based products 50% of our retail range by 2025.

Available exclusively from Waitrose.

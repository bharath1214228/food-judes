LLOYD’S BANK POSITIVE SOCIAL & SUSTAINABLE IMPACT AWARD

We won the Positive Social & Sustainable Impact award at the The Lloyd's Bank British Business Excellence Awards!  We’re so honoured and delighted that our efforts to bring life to people and the planet were recognised in this way.
 

It started with becoming a certified BCorp, reducing our carbon intensity by 20% since 2020, growing our plant based ranges and working with our amazing charity partners Resurgo Spear Programme and Home for Good.

However, we always remind ourselves this is just the beginning!  We are determined to keep doing more and more and more towards making the best tasting products in the most sustainable ways.
